Lonavala

Embrace the Serene Symphony of Lonavala.

  • DESCRIPTION
  • Map

Nestled in the Pune district of Maharashtra, India, Lonavala-Khandala stands as a picturesque hill station and a Municipal Council. Positioned approximately 64 km (40 miles) west of Pune and 96 km (60 miles) to the east of Mumbai, this serene locale holds a special place for travelers and locals alike.

Famous for its delectable hard candy, the chikki, Lonavala-Khandala also boasts significance as a pivotal stop along the Mumbai-Pune railway line. For those journeying from Pune suburbs, local trains departing from Pune Junction offer convenient access to this charming destination.

Moreover, Lonavala-Khandala finds itself intersected by two major thoroughfares—the Mumbai-Pune Expressway and the Mumbai-Bengaluru highway—making it easily accessible and a natural resting point for travelers traversing these routes. Its lush landscapes and pleasant climate beckon visitors, offering a tranquil escape amidst nature's beauty.
